About

Udaya Wijenayake received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering and M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from Kyungpook National University (KNU), South Korea, in 2018 and 2013 respectively. He obtained his B.Sc. (Honours) in Computer Science from the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC), Sri Lanka. He is currently the Head and a Senior Lecturer at the Department of Computer Engineering, University of Sri Jayewardenepura (USJ).
